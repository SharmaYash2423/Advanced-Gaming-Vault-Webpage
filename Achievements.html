Achievements

Celebrating the milestones and victories in our gaming journey.

Achievements of Gaming Industry in India in past few decades.

1. Launch of NASSCOM Gaming Forum

Establishment of NASSCOM Gaming Forum in 2008 to support and grow the Indian gaming ecosystem.

2. Success of BGMI

BGMI's massive popularity and its impact on mobile gaming in India, leading to a surge in gaming culture.

3. Indian Games on Global Platforms

Indian games like 'Asura' and 'Raji: An Ancient Epic' gaining international recognition.

4. Growth of eSports

Rapid growth of eSports in India with major tournaments and increasing participation.

5. Introduction of Indian Gaming Startups

Emergence of successful gaming startups like Nazara Technologies and Dream11.

6. Investment in Gaming

Increased investment in the Indian gaming industry, fostering growth and innovation.

7. Launch of Gaming Courses

Introduction of specialized gaming courses and programs in Indian universities.

8. Rise of Indie Game Developers

Indie game developers in India making a mark with innovative and creative games.

9. International Collaborations

Collaborations between Indian and international gaming companies, boosting the industry.

10. Government Initiatives

Government support and initiatives to promote gaming and eSports in India.
